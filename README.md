# WALCHAND-IMAGINE-CUP-2026
Walchand Imagine Cup 2026 (WIC 26) is a national-level innovation and startup challenge organized by the Microsoft Learn Students’ Club (MLSC), Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli. Inspired by the global Microsoft Imagine Cup, the event encourages students to ideate, build, and pitch technology-based solutions to real-world problems.

## About the Challenge

Walchand Imagine Cup 2026 aims to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students. The challenge encourages participants to develop technology-based solutions that address real-world problems and contribute to sustainable development.

## Key Features

- **Innovation-Driven**: Focus on creative problem-solving and technological innovation
- **Student-Centric**: Designed specifically for undergraduate and postgraduate students
- **Mentorship Support**: Access to expert mentors and industry professionals
- **Networking Opportunities**: Connect with like-minded innovators and entrepreneurs
- **Prizes and Recognition**: Attractive prizes and recognition for winning projects

## How to Participate

1. Register your team on the official WIC 26 portal
2. Develop your innovative project or startup idea
3. Submit your project with comprehensive documentation
4. Present your idea to the judges
5. Win prizes based on evaluation criteria

## Contact Information

For more information about the challenge, visit the official website or contact the organizing committee.
